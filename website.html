About AMPKNOW TECH Academy

Founded in 2023, AMPKNOW TECH Academy is a ISO Certified Academy , Our Academy has been at the forefront of providing cutting-edge technical and non-technical education to engineering students across India. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic curriculum and industry requirements through specialized training programs, internships, and mentorship.
